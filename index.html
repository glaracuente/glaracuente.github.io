About Me




Hello World. My name is Gerardo Laracuente, but everyone calls me Gerry. I've had an interest in technology my whole life. When I was a kid, I used to open up electronics and try to figure out how they worked. That curiosity is still alive in me today. I love to understand how things work, and it's a big part of my current job.


I'm a Data Center Server Engineer at Outbrain, Inc. The job title doesn't really describe what I actually do. I use DevOps principles to try to automate all the tasks I'm responsible for. I get to play around with a lot of great tools (mostly open source) such as Chef, Jenkins, Prometheus, Grafana, and Ruby/Bash scripting.


I'm currently learning Full Stack Web Development so I can be a better engineer. The next step for me is to teach myself level languages such as C++ to get a better understanding of computer science.


My ultimate goal is to use computer science and engineering to help those most in need in our society.
